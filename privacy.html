Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

1. Data Collection

Seeker Group (SG) is a decentralized application. We DO NOT collect, store, or sell any personal information, including names, phone numbers, or email addresses.

2. Wallet and Identity

Your identity is managed through your Solana wallet. We never have access to your private keys or seed phrases. All on-chain interactions are public by the nature of the blockchain.

3. Messaging Privacy

All private communications are end-to-end encrypted. We do not have the technical capability to read your messages.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Solana Mobile Stack and XMTP. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for infrastructure-level data handling.
